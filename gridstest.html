Barn Game

 Game where you get to control the paw of a huge gryphon to stomp & trample a poor wolf to death. Also includes a bunch of extra animations involving torvid and a horse. Everything from trampling and crushing to vore and watersports!

Developer: Torvid
Relase date: 2021-04-03

Download

Fair warning, because this was made in UE4 it might require a higher end PC to run smoothly. If it runs poorly even at the lowest settings there are always the video playthroughs :)




The VR versions are just the animations, and are made with Unity.
